# AISDD FAQ

## Is AISDD a prompt library?

No. AISDD is a development workflow based on persistent project specifications.

## Does AISDD require a specific AI?

No. It is vendor-independent.

## Does AISDD require a specific stack?

No. It works with any language or architecture.

## Can AISDD be used in small projects?

Yes, but it is most valuable in long-running projects.

## Why one task per cycle?

Because it reduces regressions, context load, and ambiguity.

## Why persistent docs?

Because chat history is fragile and vendor-specific.

## What happens when information is missing?

The AI must explicitly report missing information instead of guessing silently.
